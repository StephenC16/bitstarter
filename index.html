Everyone gets a front row seat

With Mee-tings attendees can:

  • Watch the presentation in real-time on their own device
  • Take notes, synchronized with each slide
  • Quickly Tweet about the presentation
  • View the Twitter feed on the presentation
  • Send questions to the presenter
  • Rate the presentation's content, and the presenter

Attendee Super Powers

  • Whos's here watching this presentation with me?
  • Is anyone here from Acme, Inc?
  • Who made that pithy twitter comment?
  • What is their LinkedIn or Facebook profile?
  • Can I arrange a quick meeting with them at the break?
  • What sessions are happening right now? After this one?
  • How do I get to that location from here?

Mee-ting puts all the event logistics and rich attendee information within an intuitive user interface. This amplifies the personal networking possibilities of the in-person event with super-powered options and opportunities.
